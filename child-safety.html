PushMeUp Child Safety Standards

Last updated: July 3, 2026

PushMeUp is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation.

What is prohibited

We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activity involving child sexual abuse material, sexual exploitation of children, grooming, trafficking, or any attempt to harm or exploit minors.

Users must not post, share, request, encourage, or distribute content that sexualizes, exploits, endangers, or harms children.

Reporting and enforcement

We provide in-app reporting tools so users can report posts, profiles, or behavior that may violate these standards. We review reports and may remove content, suspend or permanently ban accounts, and take further action where appropriate.

Reporting to authorities

If we become aware of confirmed child sexual abuse material, we will remove it and report it to the appropriate authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children or relevant regional authorities, where required by applicable law.

Contact

For child-safety concerns, contact us at safety@pushmeup.app.
