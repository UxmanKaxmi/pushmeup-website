Contact us

The fastest way to reach us is email. Write to support@pushmeup.app and we will get back to you. PushMeUp is in beta, so your questions and feedback directly shape what we build next.

Quick answers

  • Is PushMeUp free? Yes, completely free during the beta.
  • How do I join? Tap Join the beta and get set up on iPhone or Android in a couple of minutes.
